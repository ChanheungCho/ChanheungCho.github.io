Chanheung Cho

I am a Postdoctoral Research Scholar at North Carolina State University and the Science and Technologies for Phosphorus Sustainability Center. I am also a member of Baker RES-Lab.

My research focuses on environmental and resource economics, agricultural economics, and dynamic decision modeling. I am particularly interested in the dynamic management of resources under structural and observational uncertainty.

I am on the job market during the 2024-2025 academic year.
